Overview

Let's get started! The study consists of 3 sections:

  1. Learning Guidelines
    First you will learn about the strengths and weaknesses of the AI model including its accuracy for questions covering specific categories of knowledge.

  2. Practice with Feedback
    Next you will practice applying what you learned about the model’s strengths and weaknesses through a short quiz with feedback.

  3. Testing
    Finally you will be given a series of multiple choice questions pertaining to different subjects such as social sciences, elementary math problems, politics, and more. For each question you will be asked to predict whether the AI model is likely make an error. You'll then be asked about how you made this decision.

⚠️IMPORTANT⚠️

  1. As you proceed through the study, please DO NOT CLICK YOUR BROWSERS "RETURN" BUTTON or your progress may be lost.
  2. Please DO NOT SEARCH THE INTERNET or consult any outside sources to help you answer the questions otherwise the results of this study will be flawed.
  3. Please DO NOT USE LLMs OR AI TOOLS to answer any of the questions otherwise the results of this study will be flawed.
